Regional Sales Manager - East Coast

Territory: Northeast and Mid-Atlantic States

Join a Global Leader in Smart City Solutions

Ekin is a 28-year-old company operating in 40 countries with a growing team of 250+ people. We are shaping the future of smart cities and public safety with cutting-edge AI technologies.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Business or related field; Master's preferred
  • 7+ years of enterprise sales experience with government and large organizations
  • Proven track record of managing complex, long-cycle sales processes
  • Experience with smart city technologies, public safety, or government solutions
  • Strong network within East Coast government and institutional markets
  • Excellent presentation and negotiation skills
  • Territory includes: NY, NJ, PA, CT, MA, ME, VT, NH, RI, MD, DE, DC, VA, WV

Job Description

  • Develop and execute comprehensive sales strategy for the East Coast territory
  • Build relationships with state and local government officials, police departments, and municipal leaders
  • Manage complex procurement processes and government contract negotiations
  • Coordinate with federal partners and national account teams
  • Lead regional trade show participation and industry event representation
  • Manage and mentor junior sales staff within region
