Ekin Featured in ITS International: Revolutionizing Traffic Enforcement with AI-Powered Solutions

We're thrilled to announce that Ekin has been featured in ITS International, one of the world's leading transportation technology publications, for its groundbreaking innovations in traffic management and law enforcement.

The spotlight is on the Ekin Patrol G2, our flagship AI-powered mobile enforcement solution that's transforming how authorities monitor and respond to traffic violations in real time.

A New Standard in Mobile Traffic Enforcement

Described by ITS International as "a mobile traffic unit covering seven lanes," the Ekin Patrol G2 is designed to bring unparalleled situational awareness and intelligent automation to any patrol vehicle. It features:

  • Automatic Licence Plate Recognition (ALPR)
  • 360° HD video surveillance
  • Real-time analytics for speed, parking, and hotlist detection
  • No trunk space required, plug-and-play installation

This cutting-edge system gives officers real-time visibility and control, including alerts when someone approaches the vehicle and automatic backup initiation in case of threats, all while collecting high-resolution data across seven lanes of traffic.

"The Patrol G2 can alert your officers when someone approaches their vehicle… automatically initiating a call for backup in the event of trouble."ITS International

Leading the Future of Smart City Enforcement

Ekin's feature in ITS International is a major recognition of our mission: to bring AI, mobility, and real-time intelligence into everyday policing and traffic management.

We're proud to be shaping the future of urban mobility with innovative tools that empower authorities, protect citizens, and streamline enforcement with precision and automation.
